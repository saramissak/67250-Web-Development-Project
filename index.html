The original store was established in May of 2002. It was named Wicks & Beans, as the original merchandise consisted mostly of scented candles and coffee beans. We were located at 2207 Murray Ave, also in Squirrel Hill (down and across from Mineo's Pizza.)

Since then we've become mostly a Tea Specialty store with over 400 varieties of teas in stock, and a huge assortment of:

  1. teapots
  2. tea gadgets
  3. tea cozies
  4. tea wallets


We offer Tea Classes and Tea Tastings every month. We also carry over 30 types of coffee beans and some neat coffee makers!

Apart from that we also attend to the sweet tooth of our customers by stocking up on international sweets, especially around Holidays.

Whose a good Blue Monkey Tea? We are a good Blue Monkey Tea! And we are here to serve you! We changed our name, in an attempt, to brand ourselves nationwide.
